### Reproducing results

Begin with raw Excel files.
Then run, in the following order,
1. `data_preprocessing.py`
1. `tmg_params.py`
1. `tmg_stats.py`
1. `spm_analysis.py`
1. `make_tables.py`
1. `make_figures.py`
